I had a dream


“I had a dream” is a concept created by the agency allowing young people to make their dreams come true, by helping them financially and personally. JQC has helped to young men participate in the 79th Monte Carlo Rallye, helping them achieve a project close to their hearts.

EcoRace Control


EcoRace Control is one of JQ’s registered trademarks. This label includes our technical inspection and fuel consumption measurement activities. We work for environmentally friendly automotive competitions such as the Solar Race or Madrid EcoCity in Spain, as well as the Shell Eco-marathon Challenger events everywhere in the world.
Our engineers and specialists guarantee a safe event, a complete rulebook and precise and trustworthy measurements.

Shell Eco-marathon

Our agency has been working hand in hand with Shell for many years, organising Shell Eco-marathon Europe and Asia. Aside from numerous regional media and PR events , we build the urban track the students compete on, and we manage the driver challenge on track for the journalists in our own media vehicles.

RCZ R and 308 GTI VIP programs

Peugeot trusts us with organizing and managing VIP programs for their best customers and employees. At legendary racetracks such as the Nürburgring and the Paul Ricard circuit, we welcome our guests and give them the experience of a lifetime over the week-end.

“Bumble Bee” motorhome

Because of its small size, Bumble Bee can fit anywhere. The inside space is very comfortable, and its awning can welcome up to 50 people standing, and 35 to 50 people sitting. Build-up only takes 4 hours, and dismantling 2, which makes the Bumble Bee our most flexible of hospitality structures.

“Optimus” motorhome

The Optimus offers 3 possible layouts: simple awning (84m2), simple rotunda (130m2), or double rotunda (260m2). Inside is a completely equipped space and a large space at the back allows us to stock and transport plenty of equipment.

Igloo

The Igloo allows for a lot of space, and big volumes. Thanks to its unique design, it can evolve according to your needs in length. The canvas can be made to match your company’s colours, stay black, white or play with transparency. The Igloo meets the latest safety and security standards and its unique design will guarantee you stand out from the crowd!


“Ultra Magnus” motorhome

Ultra Magnus has just recently joined the rest of our vehicles, and is still needs some work done. We will be unveiling him soon.

Urban racetrack conception, safety and build

At JQ Event, we are experts in conceiving and building urban tracks as well as making them as safe as possible. We stock our own equipment in our logistics headquarters: SEVO, TecPro, AirProtek, marshal tents, signage, arches etc. We also have track marshal tents, track signage, stickers, arches and flags.
